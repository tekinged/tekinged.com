Ometochel n.
  • name of hill between Ngeremlengui and Melekeok from which (in Palauan legend) Miladeldil threw stones to mark the territories of her four children (Melekeok, Imiungs, Oreor, and Ngerebungs).
  • ometochel a olsechall er a uetech; ometochel el miich, metechii, metech a miich.
