merau-medai-merau-medai expr.
  • way of counting when dividing off string, etc. into equal portions. If final portion is merau, one will have good luck; if medai, one will have bad luck (used only on important occasions).

From root word merau v.s.
  • rich/wealthy (i.e.; having large financial resources).
  • merau a betok a ududel.
